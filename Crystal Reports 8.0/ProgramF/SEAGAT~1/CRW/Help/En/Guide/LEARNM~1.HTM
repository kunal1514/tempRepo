The User's Guide

The printed User’s Guide, included with Seagate Crystal Reports, contains instructions for typical reporting tasks as well as some very sophisticated tasks. It also contains conceptual information to help you better understand databases, reporting and the product itself. You can also find the User’s Guide in Adobe Acrobat (PDF) format in X:\ Program Files\Seagate Software\Crystal Reports\ (x being the install drive).

Online Help

You can access the online Help by clicking Seagate Crystal Reports Help from the Help menu. The online Help includes all of the information contained in the User’s Guide, as well as a description of each command, dialog box, formula function, and operator included with the program. The online Help also contains sample formulas, sample records, and group selection formulas.
