View menu

The View menu includes commands used to modify the user interface of the program. View menu commands enable you to show or hide the toolbars, zoom in and out on a report to view it at different magnifications, and to turn guidelines, the grid, and the rulers on and off. The Group Tree view can be displayed or hidden from the View menu.
