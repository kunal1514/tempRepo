Business Calendar

IsBusinessDay (date)

NextNBusinessDays (#days)

Note:    These functions assume a Business Calendar has been set up for your system. Currently, the Business Calendar is only supported in Seagate Info but not in Seagate Crystal Reports. If you are using this function in Seagate Crystal Reports, or if you have not specified a Business Calendar for your Seagate Info system, this function assumes everyday is a business day.
