Applying a predefined style

  1. Select a predefined style for the grid from the list.

    A preview of the color scheme you select appears on the right.

    Note:    The color of the style you select may not appear correctly if your screen resolution is set to 256 colors. Increase the resolution to correct this.

  2. Click Next.

    The Customize Style tab appears.
