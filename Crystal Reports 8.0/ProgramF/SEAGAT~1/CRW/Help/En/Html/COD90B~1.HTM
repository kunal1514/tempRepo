Viewing Angle command

Use the Viewing Angle command to open the Choose a Viewing Angle dialog box. From here, you can make viewing adjustments to your 3D chart, including options to rotate, pan, move, and define the length and thickness of the frame walls surrounding your 3D chart.

In the Advanced Options section of the Choose a Viewing Angle dialog box, use the features on the Rotate tab to rotate your 3D chart to the specified angle.

In the Advanced Options section of the Choose a Viewing Angle dialog box, use the features on the Pan tab to pan and zoom your 3D chart inside the chart frame.

In the Advanced Options section of the Choose a Viewing Angle dialog box, use the features on the Walls tab to increase or decrease the length and thickness of the frame walls surrounding your 3D chart.

In the Advanced Options section of the Choose a Viewing Angle dialog box, use the features on the Move tab to move the 3D chart within the chart frame.

Note:    This command is also available as a button on the Analyzer Tools toolbar.
