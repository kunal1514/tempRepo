Completing the report

  1. Click Preview Report.

    The Cross-Tab appears in the Preview tab.

  2. If you do not want the report to appear in the Report Header, click the Design tab, and move the Cross-Tab to the Report Footer, or Group Header or Footer.

Note:    If you place the Cross-Tab in the Report Footer, unsuppress the footer, by right-clicking in the Report Footer area and selecting Don't Suppress.

  1. Click the Preview tab to view the report.
