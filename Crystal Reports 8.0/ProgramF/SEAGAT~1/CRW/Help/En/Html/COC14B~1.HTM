Zoom command

Use the Zoom command to open the Magnification Factor dialog box to zoom in and out on your report. With this command you can view your report at anywhere from 25% to 400% of its actual size, make it fit within the page or reset the size to 100%.

At the lower magnifications you will see more emphasis on layout; at the higher magnifications, you will see more emphasis on details.

Note:    These options are also available using the Zoom Control on the Standard toolbar.
