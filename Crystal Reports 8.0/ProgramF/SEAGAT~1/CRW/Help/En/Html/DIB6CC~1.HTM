Seagate Software Registration Wizard

The Seagate Software Registration Wizard appears when you start Seagate Crystal Reports, or when you choose the Register/Change Address command from the Help menu. Use the Registration Wizard to register your copy of Seagate Crystal Reports.

Once you have registered your copy of the program with Seagate Software, you will receive a registration number. Once you have entered the serial number, the Registration Wizard will no longer appear on startup.

There are two good reasons for registering:

  • registration entitles you to technical support, should you ever require assistance in using the product
  • registration assures you that you will be notified whenever the product is upgraded to offer new features, benefits, and efficiencies.
