Chart command

Use the Chart command to insert a chart into your report. When you choose the Chart command, the Chart Expert appears. Use this Expert to choose from among four types of Charts:

  • a simple group chart,
  • a detail/formula chart,
  • a cross-tab chart, or
  • an OLAP chart

Follow the step-by-step instructions in the Chart Expert to create your chart.

Note:    This command is also available as a button on the Standard toolbar.
