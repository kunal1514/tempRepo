Dot Density Option view

A Dot Density map displays a dot for each occurrence of a specified item. For example, you might create a United States map that shows one dot for each national park. The purpose of a Dot Density map, then, is to provide an overall impression of the distribution of the specified item. Such a map is not a very accurate means of showing detailed information, but is a good way to give an impression of distribution.

Size of dots

Click Small or Large to define the size of the dots you want on your Dot Density map.
