Report of order data - grouped by product

This report groups the data by product. Each group displays all the orders for a specific product. At first it appears that this might be useful, but then it becomes clear that each product group includes orders from several different regions.

The information is helpful, and it brings you closer to your goal, but you are still a long way from having the information you need.

Each product group contains orders for many regions.
