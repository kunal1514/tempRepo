Conditions for summary functions

Here is a list of the possible condition text strings for the summary functions:

Boolean conditions

  • any change
  • change to Yes
  • change to No
  • every Yes
  • every No
  • next is Yes
  • next is No

Date conditions

  • daily
  • weekly
  • biweekly
  • semimonthly
  • monthly
  • quarterly
  • semiannually
  • annually

Time conditions

  • by second
  • by minute
  • by hour
  • by AMPM
