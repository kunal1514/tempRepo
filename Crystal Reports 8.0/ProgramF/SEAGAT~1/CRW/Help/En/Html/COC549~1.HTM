Map command

Use the Map command to open the Map Expert and insert a map into your report. Maps help you analyze your report data and identify trends more efficiently. For example, you could create a map that shows sales by region. You would then be able to:

  • drill down on the map regions to view the underlying data,
  • analyze the data using one of the five map types, and
  • use the Analyzer tab to adjust the appearance and organization of the map (allowing you to better identify trends).

In order to place a generic, group-based map on your report, you must first have a group and a summary or subtotal field present in the report. However, Seagate Crystal Reports does provide several specialized map layouts (Detail, cross-tab, and OLAP) that do not require groups and summaries.

Note:    The Map command is only available if you have installed the mapping component of Seagate Crystal Reports.
