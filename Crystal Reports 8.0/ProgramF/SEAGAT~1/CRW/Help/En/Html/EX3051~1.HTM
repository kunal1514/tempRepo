Map Expert

This Expert appears:

  • when you click the Insert Map button from the Standard toolbar,
  • when you choose the Map command from the Insert menu, or
  • when you right-click a Map and choose Map Expert.

The Map Expert consists of several tabs that lead you through step-by-step instructions for creating a map in your report.

  • Data tab
  • Type tab
  • Text tab
