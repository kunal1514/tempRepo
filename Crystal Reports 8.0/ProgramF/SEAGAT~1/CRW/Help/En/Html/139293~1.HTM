Hiding and showing the Map Navigator

You have the option of hiding or showing the Map Navigator.

To hide the Map Navigator
  1. On the Analyzer tab, right-click the map to bring up the shortcut menu.
  2. On the shortcut menu, click Hide Map Navigator.

    The Map Navigator is removed from the Analyzer tab.

To show the Map Navigator
  1. On the Analyzer tab, right-click the map to bring up the shortcut menu.
  2. On the shortcut menu, click Show Map Navigator.

    The Map Navigator is displayed on the Analyzer tab.
