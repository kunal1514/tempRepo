Overview

The program includes a number of Experts that help you create several kinds of reports, prepare graphs, set up the overall report format, link related databases, select records and groups for inclusion in the report, and gather the files you need to include when you distribute the report with step-by-step instructions.

In some cases, Experts have a series of tabs numbered Step 1, Step 2, and so on. To use this kind of Expert, simply begin at Step 1 and proceed to the last step. When you have completed the last step, the Expert will do the rest of the work. It's that easy!

Some of the other Experts do not use the tab system. To use this kind of Expert, simply follow the instructions in the dialog boxes that appear and the Expert will lead you through otherwise complex procedures with a minimum of effort.
