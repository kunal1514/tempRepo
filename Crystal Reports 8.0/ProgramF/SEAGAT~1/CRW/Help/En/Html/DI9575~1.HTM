Bar Chart Option view

A Bar Chart map works like a Pie Chart map, but may be more useful for certain sets of data. Typically, you would use a Bar Chart map for items that do not total 100% (that is, for data items that do not make a whole, or for data items that are unrelated).

Size of bars

Use the slider to choose Large, Medium, or Small as the size of the bars to display on your map.
