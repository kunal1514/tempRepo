Look up all of one, then all of others (A to B, A to C)

For each record in the Customer table, this option looks for all the matching records in the Credit table (Table B) and then looks for all the matching records in the Orders table (Table C). Then it repeats the process with the next record in the Customer table, and continues with the rest of the records.
