Auto-Arrange Report command

Use the Auto-Arrange command to:

  • adjust the spacing between fields,
  • reposition the fields, and then
  • center your report on the page, automatically.

When you place a field on your report, Seagate Crystal Reports allocates a space equal to the field width specified in your database. However, this field width is often far larger than the width of the values within the field. Use the Auto-Arrange command to resize and reposition the fields automatically.
