Changing width and height of Cross-Tab cells

  1. In the Design tab, click a Row or Column heading within the Cross-Tab, to activate the sizing handles.
  2. Click and drag one of the sizing handles to expand the width or height of the Cross-Tab cell.
  3. Click the Preview tab to view the changes.

Note:    Changing one row or column heading affects all of the rows or columns.
