Shortcut menus

Everywhere in Seagate Crystal Reports you can display shortcut menus, by clicking the right mouse button. These menus enable you to work more quickly by providing quick access to the commands that are relevant to what you are doing at the time. For example if you have a text object selected right-clicking brings up a shortcut menu listing all the text editing and formatting commands.
