Destination

The destination determines the export location of your report. Seagate Crystal Report enables you to choose one of six destinations. They include:

  • Application
  • Disk file
  • Microsoft Exchange folder
  • Lotus Domino
  • Lotus cc:Mail (VIM)
  • Microsoft Mail (MAPI).
