Chart Expert

The Chart Expert appears:

  • when you click the Insert Chart button on the Standard toolbar, or
  • when you choose the Chart command from the Insert menu.

Use the Chart Expert to prepare graphs for use in a report. When the Chart Expert appears, the Type tab is active.
