Changing the map title

  1. Right-click the map to bring up the shortcut menu.
  2. On the shortcut menu, click Change Map Title.
  3. In the Change Map Title dialog box, enter a new title.
  4. Click OK to save your changes.

    Seagate Crystal Reports returns you to the Analyzer tab and implements your changes.
