Sample Reports

The samples installed with Seagate Crystal Reports give you an idea of the different types of reports that can be created and how they look in the Report Designer.

The sample reports installed with Seagate Crystal Reports which demonstrate sorting, grouping and totaling are:

  • Sort.rpt
  • Sort Multiple Field.rpt
  • Group By First Letter.rpt
  • Group By Intervals.rpt
  • Group.rpt
  • Summary Group.rpt
  • Summary With Formula.rpt
  • Subtotal Group.rpt
  • Subtotal Multiple Level.rpt
  • new path markerInventory.rpt
  • Product Catalog.rpt

Sample reports are located in the Crystal Reports directory under \Samples\Reports.

Additional Sample reports are available from Seagate Software's Community web site:
