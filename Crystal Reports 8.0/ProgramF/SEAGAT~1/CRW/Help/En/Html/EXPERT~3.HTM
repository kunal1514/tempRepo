Drill tab

Use the Drill tab to summarize data even further by choosing which section you want hidden in a report.

Section

This list displays all of the sections currently in use in a report. Before the section name are the words show or hide indicating the status of the section.

  • To show a hidden section, click the section. Its Hide status will change to Show.
  • To hide a section that is showing, click the section. Its Show status will change to Hide.
