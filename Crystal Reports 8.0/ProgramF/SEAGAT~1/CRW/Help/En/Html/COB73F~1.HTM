Export command

Use the Export command to export your report in one of several popular word processing, database, and spreadsheet formats, in HTML or ODBC format, or in one of a number of standard data interchange formats. For example, you can export your report in Microsoft Excel format and then open it in Excel as a spreadsheet file.

You can export to a disk file, to an application, to a Lotus Notes database, to an Exchange Folder, or to e-mail.

Note:    This command is also available as a button on the Standard toolbar.
