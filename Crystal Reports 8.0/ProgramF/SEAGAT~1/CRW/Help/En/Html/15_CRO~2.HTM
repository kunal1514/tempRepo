To create a new cross-tab report

  1. On the File menu, click New.

    The Seagate Crystal Report Gallery appears.

  2. In the Choose an Expert area click Cross-Tab.

  3. Click OK.

    The Cross-Tab Report Expert appears. This expert is made up of seven tabs: Data, Links, Cross-Tab, Style, Customize Style, Chart, and Select.
