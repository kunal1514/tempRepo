Adding borders, color, and shading to a field

Seagate Crystal Reports allows you to add borders, color, and shading to fields on your report in order to emphasize important data and create professional-looking reports.

To add borders, color, and shading to a field
  1. Right-click the field you want to format to bring up the shortcut menu.
  2. On the shortcut menu, click Change Border.

    The Format Editor dialog box appears with the Border tab open.

  3. Select the line style, color, and background color of the field.
  4. Click OK to save your changes.
