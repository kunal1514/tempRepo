Toolbars command

Use the Toolbars command to open the Toolbars dialog box to control the appearance of the toolbars.

There are four toolbars to choose from: Standard, Formatting, Supplementary Tools, and Analyzer Tools. Use the check boxes provided to show any or all of the toolbars.

To view larger buttons on your toolbars, select the Large Buttons check box.

Tooltips are windows that appear with a name when your cursor rests on top of a button. To view tooltips on your toolbars, select the Show ToolTips check box.
