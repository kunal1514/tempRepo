Zoom In command

Select the Zoom In command to get a closer view of your chart or map. Once you activate the Zoom In mode, your cursor changes to represent a magnifying glass. The magnifying glass includes a plus sign to inform you that the selected area increases in size as you click the chart or map.

Note:    This command is also available as a button on the Analyzer Tools toolbar.
