Working with maps

Editing maps using the Map Expert

Once you have created a map, you may want to add a new title, headings, and a legend, and you may want to change fonts, or even the map type. From either the Design or Preview tab, you can open the Map Expert and make your modifications.

To edit a map using the Map Expert
  1. Right-click the map to bring up the shortcut menu.
  2. On the shortcut menu, click Map Expert.
  3. In the Map Expert dialog box, make the desired changes.
  4. Click OK.
