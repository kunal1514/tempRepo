New command

When you choose the New command the Seagate Crystal Report Gallery appears. You can create a report using one of the following report experts, or click As a Blank Report to open a blank report and create your report yourself.

  • Standard Expert
  • Form Letter Expert
  • Form Expert
  • Cross-Tab Expert
  • Subreport Expert
  • Mail Label Expert
  • Drill Down Expert
  • OLAP Report Expert
