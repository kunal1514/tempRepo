Idle Time

Idle time is a period of time during which no action occurs.

Minutes before idle job is closed

A job refers to a report that has been generated and cached on the server. If nobody requests the report for the time specified, the report job is closed and discarded. As a result, if someone requests the report after the job has been closed, a new job needs to be generated, causing an initial delay.
